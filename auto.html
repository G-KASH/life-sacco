Auto Insurance FAQs

What's the difference between comprehensive and collision?

Collision covers damage to your car from accidents with other vehicles or objects. Comprehensive covers non-collision incidents like theft, vandalism, or weather damage. Both are included in our Full Protection and Premium packages.

How does accident forgiveness work?

With our Premium package, your first at-fault accident won't cause your rates to increase. This benefit applies after 3 years of accident-free driving with us.

What should I do after an accident?

  1. Ensure everyone is safe and call 911 if needed
  2. Exchange information with other drivers
  3. Take photos of the scene and damage
  4. Contact RoadShield to start your claim

Can I get discounts?

Yes! We offer multiple discounts including:

  • Multi-vehicle (up to 25% off)
  • Safe driver (accident-free for 3+ years)
  • Anti-theft devices
  • Defensive driving courses
  • Good student (for drivers under 25)
