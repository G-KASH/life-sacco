Health Insurance FAQs

What's the waiting period for pre-existing conditions?

Our standard waiting period is 12 months for pre-existing conditions. However, this may vary based on the specific condition and plan selected.

Can I include my parents in my family plan?

Yes, our family floater plans allow you to include parents, spouse, and children up to age 26. Senior-specific plans are available for parents over 60.

How do I file a claim?

For network providers, claims are filed automatically. For out-of-network care, you can submit claims through our mobile app, website, or by mail.

Are preventive services covered?

All our plans include 100% coverage for preventive services like annual physicals, vaccinations, and cancer screenings as per guidelines.
