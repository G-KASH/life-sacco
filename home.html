Is theft included in basic coverage?

Yes, theft and burglary protection is included in all plans.

Can I cover luxury items like jewelry?

Yes, luxury items are covered under our Premium Protection plan with higher limits.

What happens if my home becomes unlivable?

Comprehensive and Premium plans cover temporary housing costs until repairs are completed.
