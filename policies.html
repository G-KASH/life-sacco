SACCO Policies & Terms

Membership Eligibility

  1. Members must be at least 18 years of age
  2. Valid government-issued ID required
  3. Minimum initial share contribution of $100
  4. Completion of membership application form
  5. Approval by the SACCO management committee

Member Responsibilities

  1. Regular attendance of general meetings
  2. Timely payment of monthly contributions
  3. Adherence to SACCO bylaws and regulations
  4. Proper utilization of SACCO funds
  5. Maintenance of accurate personal records

Financial Obligations

  • Monthly Contributions: Minimum $50 or 5% of monthly income (whichever is higher)
  • Shares: Minimum 100 shares at $1 par value
  • Loan Repayment: Maximum 24 months at 10% interest
  • Withdrawals: 30 days notice required for share withdrawals

General Terms

  1. All members are subject to these terms upon registration
  2. The SACCO reserves the right to amend terms with 30 days notice
  3. Membership may be terminated for violation of terms
  4. Disputes shall be resolved through SACCO arbitration

Loan Terms

  • Eligibility: 6 months continuous membership
  • Limit: 3 times share capital
  • Guarantors: 2 members with equal shares
  • Default: 5% penalty after 30 days

Prohibited Activities

  1. Misuse of SACCO funds
  2. Unauthorized disclosure of member information
  3. Fraudulent loan applications
  4. Failure to repay loans
  5. Disruptive behavior during meetings

Governance Policy

  1. Annual general meeting every March
  2. Board elections every 2 years
  3. Financial audits conducted quarterly
  4. Transparent record keeping

Risk Management

  • Insurance: All deposits insured up to $50,000
  • Reserves: 20% of profits retained
  • Diversification: Maximum 30% in any single investment

Member Benefits

  1. Dividends paid annually (minimum 70% of profits)
  2. Emergency loans at 5% interest
  3. Financial literacy training
  4. Discounts on insurance products

Policy Acceptance

By continuing to use our services, you acknowledge that you have read and agreed to our policies and terms.
